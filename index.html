Planning

Cyber Security attacks are increase nowadays, try to ask yourself a couple of questions firstly: What would happen if your systems were hacked? Could confidential data be at risk? What would be the financial, legal and goodwill implications of an attack?

Testing

Security Design Review, Penetration Testing and Security Consulting are the most common tasks to avoid security trouble in any I.T infrastructure by protecting and assure your application from damaging the infrastructure or costly attacks. Keep in mind: ‘Test before goes online’.

Keeping

Once yours applications are tested and ready to go online you should keep it on watching and attention to all details. New vulnerabilities are released daily and you still may be the target! Keep you application safe by reviewing the ‘security big picture’ and re-testing it when necessary.
